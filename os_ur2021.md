# Call For Papers

## A special session on Cloud Robot Intelligence

* Conference: [2021 The 18th International Conference on Ubiquitous Robots](http://www.ubiquitousrobots.org/2021/index.php)
* Venue: Lakai Sandpine Resort, Gangneung-si, Gangwon-do, Korea, 12 – 14 July, 2021

### Objectives and Motivation

This purpose of this special session is to share recent technical advancements and important issues in the joint fields of cloud robotics and artificial intelligence. We are especially interested in software frameworks and learning strategies for continually improving the intelligence level of service robots with the use of cloud-based platforms, as well as robot intelligence serving architecture to enable dynamic evolving of robot intelligence models deployed on the cloud.

### List of Topics

* Cloud-based software framework for cloud robot intelligence
* Learning methodologies for cloud robots: continual, transfer and adaptive
* Distributed learning frameworks for cloud robots including federated learning
* Methods of deploying and evolving cloud robot intelligence
* Evaluation of cloud robot intelligence

### Paper Submission Guide

We welcome regular papers as well as short work-in-progress papers.

* **Regular Papers (4-8 pages)**: Prospective authors are invited to submit high-quality papers for oral presentation, and the accepted papers will be included in Ubiquitous Robots IEEE Xplore Conference Proceedings.
* **Work-in-Progress Papers (2-4 pages)**: *The conference seeks contributions from a broad set of on-going works for poster presentation with possible active interaction in the conference. The accepted papers will be included in the conference proceedings for distribution to the conference participants. However, work-in-progress papers will not be uploaded to the IEEE Xplore Digital Library, and the authors retain the copyright.*

Authors should prepare their papers based on a template provided by IEEE ([LaTex template](http://ras.papercept.net/conferences/support/tex.php) *or* [Microsoft Word template](http://ras.papercept.net/conferences/support/word.php)). All papers must be written in English and submitted electronically in A4 PDF format. Submission links are now available on [ras.papercept.net](https://ras.papercept.net/conferences/scripts/start.pl) *(You can find UR 2021 in the middle of the list of conferences).
